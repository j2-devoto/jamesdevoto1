RIGHT DECK

1. Songs can be loaded onto each deck by clicking the deck and chosing a song from the selection

2. The large, square toggle below each deck is to start and stop the track

3. The speed of the track can be adjusted by moving the slider labelled 'pitch'

4. The six dials on the 'mixer' make changes to the equilization of each of the two tracks, as on a industy standard mixer, there are 'lows' at the bottom, 'mids' in the middle, and 'highs' at the top

5. The vinyl icon can only be used for song selection and not for adjusting the tracks speed. The two round buttons between each vinyl are used to 'jog' the track forward and backwards for beat matching

7. The volume of each track can be adjusted using the two sliders below the EQ on the mixer

8. The dials labeled 'filter' can be used to add a filter to each of the tracks

9. The dials labeled 'reverb' and 'phaser' can be used to add those effect to the relevant channel

10. Press the 'close' button under the 'help' button to minimise this window
